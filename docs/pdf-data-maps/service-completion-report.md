## What this PDF intentionally does NOT show

* **Money totals.** Pricing belongs on the Invoice. The SCR is a factual record of what was done, not a billing document.
* **Photo thumbnails.** The SCR's attachment photos live on `file_attachments` (related to the report) and are listed in the Proof Packet PDF; embedding them in this PDF needs PDF image XObject support ã not in the hand-rolled renderer.
* **Customer signature.** The customer signs the Receipt, not the SCR.

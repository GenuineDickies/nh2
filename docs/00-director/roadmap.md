# Roadmap

## Product Direction

Build a practical solo roadside command center focused on fast job capture, mobile execution, proof, payment, and clean records.

## Phase Roadmap

| Phase | Focus | Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | Project audit and setup | Documentation, project decisions, test plan, and known starting constraints. |
| 1 | Foundation data model | Core customer, vehicle, location, intake, service request, and audit structures. |
| 2 | Core UI shell | Mobile-first app shell with dashboard navigation and polished dark UI. |
| 3 | Dashboard | Daily command center with counts, alerts, and quick actions. |
| 4 | Intake | Fast call and lead capture with phone duplicate detection. |
| 5 | Service Requests | Core job record with status, related records, and timeline. |
| 6 | Catalog | Services, parts, materials, fees, and labor catalog. |
| 7 | Estimates | Professional estimates with line items, totals, disclaimers, and PDFs. |
| 8 | Customer Approvals | Approval records tied to estimates, changes, or invoice authorization. |
| 9 | Work Orders / Dispatch | Field-ready job execution with dispatch timestamps. |
| 10 | Service Completion Reports | Factual record of work performed, VIN, notes, photos, and parts. |
| 11 | Invoices | Invoice generation, validation, totals, PDFs, and posting. |
| 12 | Payments / Receipts | Payment recording, balance updates, receipts, and ledger impact. |
| 13 | Vendor Documents | Receipt upload, review, categorization, and expense posting. |
| 14 | Accounting | Chart of accounts, ledger, posting previews, and balanced entries. |
| 15 | Proof Packet | Consolidated job record package for dispute defense. |
| 16 | Reports | Revenue, margin, expenses, unpaid invoices, lead sources, and tax summary. |
| 17 | Customer Portal | Tokenized no-login customer actions. |
| 18 | Notifications | SMS and email templates, sends, and delivery status. |
| 19 | Integrations | Square, Stripe, Telnyx, Google Maps, VIN decode, and QuickBooks later. |
| 20 | Polish and Hardening | Mobile behavior, validation, empty states, PDFs, audit logs, and tests. |

## Near-Term Sprints

### Sprint 1

Establish docs, migrations, app shell, dashboard skeleton, intake, service requests, and audit logging.

### Sprint 2

Add catalog, estimates, approval records, and estimate PDF generation.

### Sprint 3

Add work orders, field workflow, completion reports, VIN/no-vehicle-serviced logic, and attachments.

### Sprint 4

Add invoices, payments, receipts, chart of accounts, ledger posting, and full job-to-cash test.

### Sprint 5

Add vendor documents, expense categorization, proof packet, and basic reports.
